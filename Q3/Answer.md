### 1. Target Customers
- Kamereo is primarily a B2B company. Kamereo serves a diverse range of businesses in the food and beverage sector, including restaurants, hotels, cafes, and bars. Recent expansion targets supermarkets, convenience stores, hospitals, and factory canteens. 

### 2. Needs and Challenges Addressed
- **Whole Supply Chain:** Kamereo has their own supply chain to help reduce communication and other costs for restaurants.
- **Transparency and Safety:** Kamereo can offer tours for businesses and provide proofs the origin of ingredients.
- **Solution for individual customers:** Kamereo also provides personal items from MRE to tissues or toilet paper.

### 3. Primary Product Categories
- Kamereo began by focusing on the most complex category: vegetables and fruits. They have since expanded into eggs, meat, dried goods, drinks, and essential items like toilet paper.

### 4. Key Performance Metrics
- Revenue Growth
- Profitability
- Customer Penetration
- Cash Flow Management

### 5. Factors for Successful Capital Raise
- **Quality focus:** Kamereo shows a clear plan toward growth and profitability.
- **Relationship Building and Trust:** Mr. Tanaka accepts that sometimes conversation, especially first contact, is not enough to build trust. The key is maintaining long-term communication with investors, even when not actively fundraising. Besides, he also invests time to build personal relationships, because sometimes co-operation is also about person-to-person connection.

### 6. New Product Categories and Development Reasons
- **Food ancillaries:** Kamereo is developing its own line of products like food wraps or tissue by partnering with local factories.
- **Fresh seafood:** Kamereo used tank in the factory to store and deliver fresh seafood.
- **Ready-to-Eat Products:** In response to busy or alone lifestyles, Kamereo is developing affordable, pre-cut, and pre-washed ready-to-eat meals.
